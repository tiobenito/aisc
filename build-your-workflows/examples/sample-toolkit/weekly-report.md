# Weekly Report

**Type:** Skill
**Trigger:** "draft my weekly report" or "weekly update"
**Time saved:** ~20 minutes/week

## What it does

Generates a structured weekly report from your notes, completed tasks, and calendar. Formats it for sharing with your manager or team -- highlights wins, flags risks, and outlines next week's priorities.

## How it works

1. Asks what you accomplished this week (or reads from a tasks file / notes)
2. Asks about any blockers, risks, or things that need attention
3. Asks what's planned for next week
4. Drafts a clean report in your preferred format
5. Keeps it concise -- aims for something you can send in a Slack message or short email

## Example output

```
Weekly Update - April 7, 2026

Wins:
- Closed vendor negotiation - saved 15% on annual contract
- Shipped onboarding automation - new hires now get day-1 access to all tools
- Team offsite logistics locked in for May

Risks / Needs attention:
- Q2 headcount still pending VP approval (2 weeks now)
- Engineering bandwidth tight - may need to delay dashboard project

Next week:
- Kick off Q2 planning with department leads
- Interview 2 candidates for ops analyst role
- Finalize offsite agenda and send to team
```
