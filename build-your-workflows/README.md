# Build Your Workflows

Turn your AI Audit into working workflows. This is your capstone -- pick 3-5 high-impact items and build them with Claude Code.

## What you'll build

A personal toolkit of 3-5 working workflows: skills, scheduled tasks, MCP integrations, or automations you'll use after the course.

## Prerequisites

- Your `my-ai-audit.md` from Week 1 (or a list of tasks you want to automate)
- Completed at least one Week 2 and one Week 3 exercise

## How to use it

```bash
git clone https://github.com/tiobenito/aisc build-your-workflows
cd build-your-workflows/build-your-workflows
claude
```

Claude will coach you through picking, building, and organizing your workflows.

## What's inside

- `CLAUDE.md` -- your coach (Claude reads this automatically)
- `examples/sample-toolkit/` -- example of what a completed toolkit looks like
- `my-toolkit/` -- where your workflows go
